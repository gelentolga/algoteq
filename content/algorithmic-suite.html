Algoteq Algorithm Suite

The Algoteq Algorithm Suite is built on a multi-tier model, integrating low-level trading tactics with high-level strategies to deliver customisable and adaptive trading solutions across asset classes.

Trading Behaviour

Our framework abstracts human and machine trading actions into coded units for flexible strategy composition. Algorithms are tuned via urgency and sensitivity parameters to align with client objectives while maintaining control and safety limits.

Data and Analysis

A curated set of derived historical data drives algorithm decisions and default settings. Real-time analytics support adaptive behaviour, smart routing, and intraday performance tracking with synthetic venues and calculated enhancements.

Available Algorithms

Our suite includes profile (VWAP, TWAP, IS, POV, MC), passive (PEG, ICEBERG), responsive (SNIPER, TOUCH, STOP), and bespoke liquidity seeking algorithms. All types offer configurable execution styles across trading scenarios.

Simulation

Simulate production and experimental algorithms on historical market data using highly parallel hardware. Visualise behaviour, test alternative strategies, and refine logic based on performance and parameter responsiveness.

Summary

The Algoteq Suite is a powerful, modular ecosystem supporting continuous development, simulation, and optimisation—ensuring robust algorithmic performance even in fast-evolving markets.
